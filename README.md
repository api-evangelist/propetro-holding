# ProPetro Holding (propetro-holding)

ProPetro Holding Corp (NYSE: PUMP) is a Midland, Texas-based oilfield services company that "provides hydraulic fracturing and other complementary services to leading upstream oil and gas companies engaged in the exploration and production of North American unconventional oil and natural gas resources," operating principally in the Permian Basin. Service lines include hydraulic fracturing, the FORCE electric fleet (eFleet), Silvertip Wireline, cementing, PROPWR power solutions, and Aqua Prop water management. ProPetro does not publish a public developer API, OpenAPI specification, SDK, or technology developer portal.
